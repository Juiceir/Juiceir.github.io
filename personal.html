Career Philosophy

“If you can't fly then run, if you can't run then walk, if you can't walk then crawl — but whatever you do, you have to keep moving forward.” Martin Luther King Jr.

This philosophy resonates deeply with me. Throughout my journey, I’ve faced setbacks both big and small, challenges in my work, relationships, and even within myself. This quote reminds me that progress is not always dramatic. Sometimes the most important thing you can do is take even the smallest step forward. When everything feels like it’s pushing against you, choosing to move in any capacity is an act of strength. This mindset has shaped how I grow, learn, and how I choose to approach every obstacle in my career.
